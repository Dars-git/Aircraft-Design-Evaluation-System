            Welcome to the AIAA Plane Designer!
            Here is an Explaination of the Website, Navigation, and Features
            Sensitivity Study
                Features:
                Allows a user to seemlessly enter a range of inputs using intuitive slider inputs.
                Generates a sensitivity study plot based on the variables entered by the user.
                To Use:
                1. Adjust the five sliders to your desired input.
                2. Press the "Submit Configuration" Button to generate the sensitivity plot
            Calculator
                Features:
                Provides users with a variety of graphing calculators which are accessible using the labeled buttons provided.
                Flying Wing Design Calculator:
                    Features: 
                    Provided with the requested inputs generates a corresponding dimension for the plane wing.
                    A save/overwrite button to save your most recent plane dimension inputs.
                    A load button to bring back your previously saved inputs.
                    To Use:
                    1. Enter the requested inputs using the form-fill input interface.
                    2. Press the "Submit Configuration" Button to confirm your choices.
                    3. To save press the "Save" button to keep your submitted configuration dimensions.
                    4. To Load press the "Load" button to reload your saved dimensions.
                Standard Wing Design Calculator:

            Log In/Register
                Features:
                Given the requested credentials, creates an account for the user or logs the user in if the credentials match in the database.
                To Use:
                1. Press the menu key in the top right corner.
                2. Press the log in button in the menu.
                3. Enter the relevant credentials and press log in or press register and enter your credentials there to create an account.
